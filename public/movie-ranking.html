Best Movies in Recent Memory According to Javon

My Top 3 in No Particular Order.

10 Cloverfield Lane (2016)

A really good Sci-Fi thriller that had me completely invested in the story.

The Substance (2024)

Surprisingly good body horror/dark comedy that left me in suspense through the entire film!

Everything Everywhere All At Once (2022)

A fantastic ride that featured a little bit of every genre from Martial Arts to Dark comedy and Action-Adventure!
